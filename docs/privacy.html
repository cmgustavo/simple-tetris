Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Simple Tetris is built to respect your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking technologies.

Data on your device

  • Game state & scores: Saved locally on your device so you can continue where you left off.
  • Settings: Theme and preferences are stored locally.

When you contact us

If you email us for support, we will use your message only to respond to you. We do not add you to mailing lists or share your contact details.

Children’s privacy

Simple Tetris is a general‑audience game and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If our practices change, we will update this page. Your continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.
